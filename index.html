Our Manifesto

01. Purpose

Founded in 2008, ONYX emerged from a simple conviction: architecture should serve humanity while respecting the earth. We are a collective of 32 architects, engineers, and dreamers spread across three continents.

02. Sustainability

Every material, every angle, every decision considers its impact on the planet. We design for generations, not trends. Our buildings are carbon-conscious, energy-efficient, and deeply connected to their natural surroundings.

03. Function

Beauty without purpose is mere decoration. Our spaces work—intuitively, efficiently, joyfully. We obsess over how people move through spaces, how light falls, how sound travels, creating environments that enhance daily life.

04. Timelessness

We reject the fleeting. Our designs transcend eras, avoiding stylistic gimmicks that date within years. Through honest materials, proportional harmony, and restrained details, we create architecture that grows more beautiful with age.
